# Focus OS — Design Context

## Audience
Solo creator / entrepreneur. One person building a business online. Uses it daily for personal goal tracking and deep work sessions — at their desk, mostly evenings.

## Brand personality
Precise · Disciplined · Relentless

## Vibe
Calm & surgical. No noise, no color, pure signal — like a professional trading terminal or Bloomberg readout. Monochrome, precise, zero clutter.

## Color
No accent color. Pure mono. Achromatic dark palette with a near-invisible cool tint (oklch chroma ~0.005, hue 260°) for subconscious coherence. Dark theme only.

## Typography
Red Hat Display — technical grotesque, distinctive numeral forms, not in reflex-reject list. Sharp at small sizes, strong hierarchy through weight alone.

## Key principles
- Every element earns its place
- Numbers are the UI — make them read cleanly (tabular-nums everywhere)
- No colored icon badges, no accent bars on cards
- Sidebar is structural, not decorative
- Motion should be invisible (instant or near-instant)
